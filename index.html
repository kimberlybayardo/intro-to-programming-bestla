Housing Program Coordinator

As the Housing Coordinator my ultimate goal was to assist clients in achieving their goals while assisting them on becoming self-sufficient. While simultaneously working alongside clients, I was building and developing the housing program that would assist clients to get their basic needs met as well as secure long term housing. Below is a brief list of what i did in my time as the housing coordinator.
  • Complete intakes, process client applications, and coordinate interviews for the housing program.
  • Monitor cleanliness and conduct monthly cleanliness inspections while also checking client progress in housing.
  • Follow all legal and ethical guidelines per state and county requirements as well as maintain all program Policies & Procedures in accordance with North County housing laws.
  • Assist with program design, service delivery, policy, and procedure development while also maintaining accurate case records and program statistics in compliance with funding requirements and objectives.

  • Assist survivors with intensive case management including; safety planning, crisis intervention, housing assistance, medical and dental care, job and life-skills training, and legal assistance through in-house programs and by referrals
